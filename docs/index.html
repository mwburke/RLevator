Exploring elevators with reinforcement learning

By: Matthew Burke

RL Elevator Agents

While academic research in reinforcement learning is continuing to advance, companies are still struggling to find practical applications in which it can be used. I believe elevators are an area of opportunity in that they have a very limited action space and environment that can be modeled fairly simply compared to high fidelity simulations that self-driving cars require. Additionally, the speed of elevators and relatively low impact of incorrect actions makes them low-risk for an implementation in a real building.
